**Engineering the Future.**

## Brand Colors
All digital and print materials must strictly adhere to the Exotic Design System token scale:

- **Primary Blue:** `#2D8CFF` (Used for primary actions, glowing accents, and emphasis)
- **Glow Accent:** `#56A8FF` (Used for hover states and gradients)
- **Surface Dark:** `#050608` (Used for card backgrounds and elevated containers)
- **Background Deep:** `#020305` (The infinite canvas; default page background)
- **Primary Text:** `#FFFFFF` (Headings and primary content)
- **Secondary Text:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.6)` (Body paragraphs and subheadings)

## Typography
- **Primary Typeface:** `Inter` (Variable)
- **Usage:** Tracking must be tight (`tracking-tight`) for Display and H1-H3 to create a compact, modern feel. Body text must utilize a relaxed line height for maximum legibility.

## Logo Usage Guidelines
1. **Clear Space:** The logo must always have a minimum clear space equivalent to the height of the "E" in Exotic on all sides.
2. **Distortion:** Never stretch, skew, or rotate the logo.
3. **Color Alteration:** The logo must only appear in pure White (`#FFFFFF`) against dark backgrounds, or in Primary Blue (`#2D8CFF`). Do not apply unauthorized gradients.
4. **Backgrounds:** The logo should only be placed on high-contrast backgrounds (preferably `#020305`). Do not place the logo over busy imagery without a dark overlay.
