## Brand Identity
- **Logo usage**: Maintain minimum clear space; do not distort or alter colors.
- **Color palette**: Primary Blue (#2D8CFF), Glow (#56A8FF), Surface (#050608), Default Background (#020305).
- **Typography**: Inter (Variable), strict tracking on Display/Heading fonts, relaxed line-height on Body.
- **Voice & Tone**: Confident, technical, precise, premium, and forward-looking.
- **Design Principles**: Refer to `DESIGN_PRINCIPLES.md`.
