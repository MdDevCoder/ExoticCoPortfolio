# Technical Discovery Call Questionnaire

*To be completed by the Lead Architect during the initial client discovery phase.*

## 1. Business Objectives
- What is the primary business goal of this product (e.g., Lead generation, B2B SaaS revenue, internal efficiency)?
- Who is the primary target audience/user base?
- What is the anticipated launch date and why?

## 2. Technical Architecture
- Do you have an existing tech stack, or are we building greenfield?
- If existing, what technologies are currently in use?
- What are the anticipated traffic volumes (Daily Active Users, concurrent requests)?
- Do you require SEO indexing (Server-Side Rendering / Static Site Generation), or is this a gated dashboard?

## 3. Data & Integrations
- Where will the data live (PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Supabase, etc.)?
- What third-party APIs need to be integrated (Stripe, Twilio, OpenAI, Web3 Wallets)?
- Are there strict compliance requirements (HIPAA, GDPR, SOC2)?

## 4. Design & UX
- Do you have existing wireframes or high-fidelity Figma designs?
- Are you comfortable utilizing the Exotic Design System, or do you require completely bespoke visual tokens?
- Are there any competitor platforms you admire visually?

## 5. Deployment & Maintenance
- Do you have a preferred cloud provider (Vercel, AWS, GCP)?
- Who will be responsible for maintaining the application post-launch? Do you require an ongoing Exotic Co. retainer?
